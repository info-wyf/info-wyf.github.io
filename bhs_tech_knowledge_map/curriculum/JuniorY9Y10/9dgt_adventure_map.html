💡 Big Idea Module 1: Artificial Intelligence
How do machines learn patterns from data to make decisions — and what are the limits?
AI systems learn from examples, not instructions. They find patterns in data that humans might miss. But they can also make mistakes, show bias, and lack true understanding. This module explores how AI works on the surface, and why we need to think critically about its outputs.
