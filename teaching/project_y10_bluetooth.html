Project Overview

In this project, students build a Bluetooth speaker to develop skills in electronics and teamwork. They learn to assemble a circuit using a pre-made PCB, connecting components like a Bluetooth audio module, speakers, and a volume potentiometer. The focus is on practical skills like soldering, wire stripping, and circuit assembly, alongside understanding how Bluetooth audio technology works.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand Bluetooth audio technology and its applications
  • Develop soldering and circuit assembly skills
  • Enhance teamwork and problem-solving abilities
